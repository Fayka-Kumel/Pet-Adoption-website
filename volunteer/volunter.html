Volunteer opportunities

find the perfect role that matches your interest and role

Pet Caretaker

Help with daily care, feeding, and playtime with our adorable pets.

Dog Walker

Take our energetic dogs for walks and outdoor activities.

Event Coordinator

Assist in organizing adoption events and fundraisers.

Foster Parent

Provide temporary homes for pets awaiting adoption.

Social Media Helper

Help showcase our pets online and manage social media.

Adminstrative Support

Assist with paperwork, scheduling, and office tasks.

Volunteer Requirements

Simple qualifications to join our team

  • Must be 18 years or older (16+ with parental consent)
  • Ability to follow instructions and work as part of a team
  • Physical ability to handle pets safely
  • Genuine love for animals and commitment to their welfare
  • Reliable and able to commit to a regular schedule
  • Pass a background check (for certain roles)
